ABOUT THE EVENT

The Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) is a government initiative under that provides skill training and placement services the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) to rural youth aged 15-35, particularly those from poor households. Launched on September 25,2014, the scheme aims to enhance the skills of rural youth for wage employment, diversify household income, and promote sustainable, long-term careers through high-quality, demand-driven training and post-placement support.
